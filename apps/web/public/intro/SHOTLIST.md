# Opening Cinematic — Motion Comic Production Map

Target runtime: 90–105 seconds  
Master canvas: 3840×2160, sRGB  
Visible safe area: center 80% (10% motion bleed on every edge)  
Art direction: stylized painterly 3D matching the supplied promotional art and character sheets, with a darker cinematic grade: wet stone, ruined greenery, dirty amber industrial light, cyan/purple Relic energy, deep blue-black shadows.

## Character anchors

- Warrior: blue hair, simple battered dark armor, red-brown underlayer; serves as the Vanguard in narration.
- Trickster: short indigo hair, teal tunic, brown leather harness and pouches; Arcane affinity.
- Engineer: dark tied-back hair, brass goggles, tool-heavy industrial armor; Force/tech affinity.
- Druid: green hair, worn green robes and vine motifs; nature affinity.

Keep silhouettes, hair colors, outfits, proportions, and signature accessories consistent with the supplied character sheets. Do not age up the cast or shift them toward photorealism.

## Layer production rules

- Every layer for a panel uses the same 3840×2160 canvas and exact origin.
- Empty areas are transparent; no layer is tightly cropped to its subject.
- Background plates must contain reconstructed artwork behind separated subjects so parallax never reveals holes.
- Extend paint at least 8% beyond the intended visible crop wherever practical.
- Keep faces, the Relic, and important hand actions inside the center 80% safe area.
- Use lossless PNG during layer production and review; export final alpha layers as lossless WebP only after edge validation.
- Never bake subtitles, narration, HUD, letterboxing, or title text into the panels.

## Recommended build order

1. Approve one flat style frame for Panel 07, because it tests all four character identities, the darker grade, rain, the pod, and the new world tone in one image.
2. Generate flat masters for Panels 01, 04, 07, 09, and 12.
3. Split and reconstruct the four hero masters into aligned layers.
4. Generate the remaining flat panels using the approved masters as continuity references.
5. Export WebP deliverables and validate all parallax movement at maximum intended offsets before wiring timing.
