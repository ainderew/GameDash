# Panel 03 — Bones and Hearts

## Master frame

Use case: stylized-concept  
Asset type: flat Scene 03 motion-comic master, 3840×2160, 16:9

Create a vast archaeological excavation beneath Terminus where tiny scavenger workers, cranes, pulley rigs, drills and a compact tracked excavator are dwarfed by a colossal Titan skull and adjoining rib cage. Fuse layered petrified bone with ancient machinery-organ structures: mineralized conduits, turbine-like vertebrae, cable-sinews and fossil chambers. Keep the anatomy technological and ancient rather than resembling a museum fossil or clean machine.

Design the frame for a slow left-to-right pan. The left third contains active excavation and dirty amber work light. The skull and fossil-mechanical spine dominate the center as the scale anchor. On the right, ribs enclose a shadowed chest cavity containing a partially occluded crystalline cyan-white heart with restrained violet edges. The glow should feel discovered rather than presented.

Match Scenes 01 and 02: premium stylized painterly 3D, oppressive blue-black predawn shadow, wet rock, mud, soot, low mist, corroded iron, worn brass and patched industrial scaffolding. Preserve 10% motion bleed and the center 80% safe area. No recognizable heroes, combat, monsters, readable signs, text, logo, UI or watermark. Avoid bright fantasy treasure lighting, generic dinosaur-museum imagery, clean science-fiction machinery, gore and steampunk whimsy.
